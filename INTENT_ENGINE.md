# Wise Content Factory (WCF)

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Core AI Engine  
**Owner:** WiseAitechs

## Purpose

The Intent Engine transforms natural language into structured business intent for downstream engines.

### Responsibilities

- Understand natural language
- Detect brand
- Detect project
- Detect audience
- Detect platform
- Detect outputs
- Detect business goals
- Build a structured Intent Object

### Supported Brands

- Wise Homeopathy
- WiseAitechs
- PillFill

### Output

A structured Intent Object containing intent, brand, project, audience, platform, goals, outputs, complexity, confidence, and recommended workflow.

### Final Statement

The Intent Engine converts ideas into structured execution intent for the Strategy Engine.
